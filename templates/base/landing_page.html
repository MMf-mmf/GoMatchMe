Frequently asked questions

Is ShidduchMe a Non-Profit Organization?

Yes, ShidduchMe is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving our community. Every penny we receive goes directly towards basic website maintenance and the development of innovative tools. We're committed to creating groundbreaking solutions that our community truly deserves, Our goal is to help you make more informed decisions, and ultimately increase success rates in creating lasting connections, all while operating as a non-profit entity.

Is my data secure on ShidduchMe?

Absolutely. We prioritize data security and privacy. Our platform uses state-of-the-art encryption and follows strict data protection regulations. We never share your information with third parties, ensuring the confidentiality and trust that is crucial in the matchmaking industry.

Who can view a single's profile on ShidduchMe?

Only verified Shadchanim can view singles' profiles on ShidduchMe. All Shadchanim on our platform undergo a thorough background check to ensure they are trustworthy and qualified to view any single's profile on the site.
