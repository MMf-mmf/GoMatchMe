Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 29, 2024

1. Introduction

ShidduchMe ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all users of our matchmaking platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information in accordance with both secular and halachic principles.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, age, contact details)
  • Religious background and observance level
  • Educational and professional information
  • Family background information
  • References and recommendations
  • Profile preferences and matching criteria
  • Communication records between users and shadchanim
  • Bounty-related transaction information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your matchmaking profile
  • To facilitate appropriate matches and suggestions
  • To process and manage bounty transactions
  • To enable communication between users and shadchanim
  • To improve our platform's functionality and user experience
  • To ensure compliance with our community standards
  • To send important updates about our service

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We maintain strict confidentiality standards and share information only:

  • With verified shadchanim who have agreed to our privacy terms
  • With potential matches, only after appropriate authorization
  • With references provided by the user
  • As required by law or to protect rights and safety

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data storage and transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Strict access controls for administrative users
  • Secure backup systems

6. User Rights and Controls

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Update or correct your profile information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Control visibility settings of your profile

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Upon account deletion, we will securely remove your personal information, except where required by law to retain certain records.

8. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at support@ShidduchMe.com.

9. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes via email or platform notification. Continued use of ShidduchMe after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
