"Bounty" is our take on the term "shadchanus gelt." The bounty will be credited to the account of the selected individual. Rest assured, the full pledge amount will only be charged once the match is confirmed. A small fee of $5 USD will be charged to your active card once every six months to keep your bounty active. All activation charges are non-refundable and will count towards your bounty pledge. Upon confirmation that a match has been successfully facilitated by the shadchan, the bounty will be securely transferred to them.

Suggestions are seen by all active shadchonim. To view a list of the active shadchonim, head over to Shadchan list. In addition, if you tag a shadchan when making a suggestion, we can guarantee that that shadchan will see the suggestion.
